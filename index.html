I am a freelance writer with bylines in the Huffington Post, the i paper and the Metro. I write non-fiction & fiction about a variety of topics, including mental health, sexuality and the environment.

I live in Oxford, UK and my pronouns are they/them.

Background

I grew up near Skipton in West Yorkshire then went to Warwick University to study Discrete Mathematics. I worked as a Java software developer for over 5 years before starting freelance writing in 2019.
